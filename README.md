#### Featured projects

**[Earnings Reaction Lab](https://github.com/ChistoOleg/ML-project)** — *Why do identical earnings beats produce opposite price reactions?*
An event-study and causal-ML pipeline over ~1,800 S&P 500 earnings events: double-selection
lasso, causal forests (CausalForestDML), and gradient boosting under purged, embargoed
cross-validation. Key finding — pre-earnings run-up is the mechanism behind "beats but
falls," and the reaction turns out to be explainable in structure but, honestly, not
predictable in magnitude.

**[Market Intelligence Bot](https://github.com/ChistoOleg/market-intel-bot)** — *A deployed, self-hosted Telegram service.*
Scheduled cross-asset market digests and per-ticker news alerts with an LLM analysis bar
(sentiment, impact, why-it-matters, what-to-watch). Single-tenant, owner-locked, 36 tests,
continuous integration, Dockerised.
